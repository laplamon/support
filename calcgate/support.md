# CalcGate Support

Welcome to CalcGate support.

CalcGate is an app that helps you lock selected apps until you solve a math challenge correctly. It is designed to make unlocking more intentional by requiring a series of correct answers instead of a simple tap.

## Main Features

- Lock selected apps or app categories
- Configure math difficulty
  - number of digits
  - math operations
  - number of questions
- Unlock only after answering all questions correctly
- Reset progress after a wrong answer
- Prevent settings changes while the lock is active

## How to Use

1. Open **Settings** in the app
2. Choose:
   - difficulty
   - operations
   - number of questions
   - restricted apps
3. Return to the home screen
4. Tap **Start Lock**
5. When you want to unlock, tap **Solve to Unlock**
6. Complete the math challenge successfully to remove the lock

## Important Notes

- FamilyControls permission is required for app restriction features
- You cannot change challenge settings while the lock is active
- If you answer a question incorrectly, your current progress is reset
- Current settings are stored locally on your device

## Troubleshooting

### The app asks for permission
CalcGate requires FamilyControls authorization to manage restricted apps. Please grant permission when prompted.

### I cannot change settings
If the lock is currently active, settings are intentionally disabled until the lock is removed.

### The lock does not start
Please make sure:
- FamilyControls permission has been granted
- at least one app or category has been selected
- the app is not already in a locked state

### I closed the app during a challenge
The app is designed to return to the home screen when reopened, rather than restoring an incomplete challenge screen.
